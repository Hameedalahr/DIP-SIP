About Dip & Sip

Your favorite destination for delicious treats at RGMCET College Expo. We bring you the perfect blend of flavors and fun!

Our Story

Dip & Sip was born from a passion for creating delightful treats that bring joy to people's lives. What started as a small idea has grown into a beloved stall at the RGMCET College Expo.

We believe in using only the finest ingredients to create our signature treats, ensuring that every bite is a memorable experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, delicious treats that bring smiles to our customers' faces while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and service.

  • Quality ingredients
  • Excellent service
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Hygienic preparation

Our Values

  • Quality First
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Innovation
  • Sustainability

We believe in creating a positive impact through our business while delivering the best possible experience to our customers.
